<h2 align="center">About Me</h2>

>[!IMPORTANT]
> Hey, I'm **Joaquin Coaquira**  
>
> I’m a **Mechanical / Product Engineer** focused on **high-power thermal systems, electro-mechanical design, and manufacturable product development**.  
>
> I specialize in taking systems from **concept → simulation → validation → production**, with strong emphasis on:
> - **Thermal management (liquid cooling, high heat flux systems)**
> - **Electro-thermal design (resistors, power dissipation systems)**
> - **Tolerance-driven mechanical design & sealing strategies**
>
> My work sits at the intersection of **physics, simulation, and real-world constraints** — where performance, cost, and manufacturability must all align.  
>
> Outside of core engineering, I explore **automation, simulation workflows, and AI-assisted design systems** to push toward more intelligent engineering processes.
