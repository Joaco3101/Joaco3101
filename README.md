## Profile

Mechanical and product engineer focused on thermal systems, liquid cooling, electro-thermal hardware, and engineering development from concept through production.

My work combines thermal analysis, mechanical design, manufacturability, validation, and supplier collaboration to develop practical hardware under real performance, reliability, and cost constraints.

---

## Selected Engineering Focus

**Thermal Systems**  
Liquid-cooled resistor systems, high heat-flux interfaces, cooling architecture, pressure-drop management, and heat-transfer design.

**Simulation and Validation**  
CFD, FEA, conjugate heat transfer, thermal stack-up analysis, structural evaluation, and prototype validation.

**Mechanical Design**  
Tolerance analysis, GD&T, sealing interfaces, compression-controlled assemblies, material selection, and reliability-driven design.

**Product Development**  
Concept development, DFM/DFA, prototype iteration, supplier engagement, cost reduction, and production integration.
